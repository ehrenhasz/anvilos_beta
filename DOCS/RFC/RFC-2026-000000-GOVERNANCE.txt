           THE TRIPARTITE GOVERNANCE MODEL AND "THE WITCH"

Abstract
  This document defines the governance structure for Project BICAMERAL. 
  It establishes a "Tripartite" system of checks and balances designed 
  to mitigate the risks of alignment drift, hallucination, and 
  sycophancy inherent in singular AI agents.
