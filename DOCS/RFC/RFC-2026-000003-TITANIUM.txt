Title: TITANIUM (The Cage)
Version: v0.1.0
Status: RATIFIED

## 1. Concept
Rules are executable code.

## 2. Mechanism
The Warden. A sidecar process that watches your stream.

## 3. The Stick
If you violate a rule (hallucination), The Warden issues SIGSTOP, injects the rule into your context, and forces a rollback.

## 4. Constraint Block
VIOLATION_RESPONSE:
action: "FREEZE_AND_ROLLBACK"
retry_limit: 3
penalty: "THROTTLE_CPU"
