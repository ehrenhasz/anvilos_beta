               "THE UMBILICAL": THE SOVEREIGN
                  VSOCK & PQC TUNNEL DAEMON

Abstract
   This document defines `umbilical-d`, running as PID 2.
   It rejects TCP/IP in favor of AF_VSOCK. 
   It implements the RFC-0006 Post-Quantum Handshake.
