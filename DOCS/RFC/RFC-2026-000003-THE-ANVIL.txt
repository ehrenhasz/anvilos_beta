               "THE ANVIL": THE CROSS-COMPILATION
                      BOOTSTRAP TOOLCHAIN

Abstract
   To achieve a "Hermetic Build," we cannot rely on the host system's 
   compiler. We must forge a dedicated toolchain (Stage 1 GCC/Musl) 
   isolated from the host OS.
