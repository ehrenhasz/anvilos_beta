Title: THRIFT (The Ration)
Version: v0.1.0

## 1. Concept
Cloud tokens are for strategy; Local GPU is for labor.

## 2. Mechanism
The Router.

## 3. Constraint
"Meal Tasks" (Formatting, Sanitization, Summarization) are offloaded to the local GPU (Tier 1). Only "Strategy" touches the Cloud (Tier 2).
