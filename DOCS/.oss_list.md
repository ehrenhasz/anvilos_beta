This document outlines the principles of Open Source Software (OSS) Sovereignty within the Anvil OS project. OSS Sovereignty ensures that Anvil OS remains independent, auditable, and controllable by its users and maintainers. It emphasizes the use of permissively licensed software and clear understanding of the software supply chain.
